Leonel Palma

I'm a creative website Designer & Developer

Hello! I’m Leonel, a digital/graphic designer with a passion for minimalist design. Pattern lover, hunter and maker, crazy about bright and neon colors. A good friend of Illustrator, because vectors are fun! Believer in life-long learning and that you can achieve anything you want if you put enough energy and time into it! I genuinely care about every project I do and that is what drives me to continuously learn and improve on the way.

My work reflects my will to create things that inspire and are memorable, love to help brands positioning by creating a unique/distinctive experience whether its about the identity, packaging, website or Ads Campaign.

I love drawing, writing, reading and helping all those who accept my support. I use that artistic methods as a tool for communicating ideas and thoughts and try to keep an open mind and i learn from people and from my own self.

what i do.

web design

Working to your brief, I can create custom designs and interfaces based on your preferences. Whether you're looking for just a website design or full web development, I can provide creativity and the technical ability to deliver excellent results.

development

If you're looking for someone to convert existing designs into fully responsive websites, I can help. I can work with a number of systems including (but not limited to) WordPress, Perch and Umbraco - all hand written with no use of themes (unless you require it!)

Graphic design

We, graphic designers create visual concepts, by hand or using computer software, to communicate ideas that inspire, inform, or captivate consumers. We develop the overall layout and production design for advertisements, magazines, and corporate reports.
